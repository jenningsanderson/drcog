Colorado Building Import

The Denver Regional Council of Governments (DRCOG) has purchased over 1M building footprints for the greater Denver area. We're going to add them all to OpenStreetMap!

Why are we doing this?

OpenStreetMap is an open map of the world that anyone can edit and use freely. In support of open data, The Denver Regional Council of Governments is making available over 1M building footprints with 6cm accuracy for importing to OpenStreetMap.
